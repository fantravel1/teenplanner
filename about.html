Empowering Teens to Plan, Grow, and Thrive

TeenPlanner is a free, friendly, and modern planning toolkit designed to help teens take control of their goals, habits, and daily life. Our mission is to make organization, self-care, and growth accessible and inspiring for everyone.

Meet the Creator

Hi! I’m John, a student and productivity enthusiast. I built TeenPlanner to help fellow teens balance school, hobbies, and wellness in a fun, stress-free way. I believe everyone deserves tools that are beautiful, empowering, and easy to use.

Let’s build better habits and reach our dreams together!
